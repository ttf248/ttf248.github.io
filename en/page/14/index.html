Hong Kong stock futures basics

As financial markets evolve, investors are seeking more effective investment tools to enhance returns. To meet this demand, Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing Limited (HKEX) has launched a series of stock futures contracts representing stocks listed on its wholly-owned subsidiary, Hong Kong Joint Stock Exchange Limited (Joint Stock). These contracts offer high liquidity and active trading, allowing investors to participate in the performance of individual companies while benefiting from features like short selling and leverage available in derivative markets.

Investors can choose to invest in stock futures representing specific industries if they believe that industry’s performance will outperform or underperform the overall stock market
