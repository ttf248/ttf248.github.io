However, the intraday trading did not follow a straight line. From morning to early afternoon, MiniMax-W’s decline was once deeper; in the afternoon it rebounded, while Zhipu continued to slide lower, ending the day with a larger drop.

In the same week, the two companies also faced newly unlocked shares and new financing arrangements. There are three different prices here: the marginal transaction price in the secondary market on that day, the price for placing new shares with specific investors, and the price at which convertible bonds may be converted into shares in the future. Mixing them into a single “market valuation” will distort the conclusion.
