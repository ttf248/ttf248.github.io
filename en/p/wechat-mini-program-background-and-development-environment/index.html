Introduction to and Preparation for WeChat Mini Programs

Why do WeChat mini programs exist?

  • Improved experience: Slow loading of embedded webpages, blank screens; Native app experiences offer faster loading
  • Standards and Management: For WeChat, accessing and managing

Before the release of mini-programs, WeChat launched a bold_italicized feature exposing its native capabilities—WeChat Pay and Wechat Cards. However, developers used web development languages, allowing them to bypass some of WeChat’s regulations. Mini-programs have their own descriptive language.

What is a mini program?

Mini-programs are applications that can be used without needing to be downloaded or installed, realizing the dream of app 触手可及

Users can open the application by scanning a QR code or searching, which also embodies the 用完即走 concept

unobtrusive

The difference between mini-programs and mobile apps

No installation, no memory usage, easy sharing: Scan code, mini-program card, search

What can mini programs do?

  • Content Tools: Zhihu Trending, Weibo Hot Topics, Mobike, Jinri Toutiao, Tencent Map, Tencent Translate
  • Retail: Pinduoduo, JD Shopping, Mogujie, Meili Fresh, Xiaomi Mall, Watson’s
  • Games: Jump, Happy Landlord, Happy Mahjong, Douyu Live Streaming, YY Live Streaming

The course content is from 2018, and some of the applications mentioned have since closed down

Development preparation

  1. Register for a mini-program account: Fill in the information as required, then click the activation link in your email
  2. Information Registration
  3. Log in to the Mini Program Management Console
  4. Complete mini-program information
  5. Binding Developer: Individual developers – the WeChat account used for login is the administrator account, no additional binding is required

Email addresses have limitations, so a new one is needed. However, INLINE_CODE_0 email allows for aliases, and WeChat’s backend doesn’t verify them. I tried it out; the mini-program name was tricky, but anything involving trademarks easily fails review.

Service categories can be selected or customized, with up to five categories per mini-program

You can view information about the mini-program and enable message push within settings. Enabling message push allows you to use message templates.

Developer Tools (A Personal Account)

Normal download and installation, no special notes. Briefly understand, enter directly in visitor mode. To enable phone debugging (viewing the development version of the mini-program), log in to the mini-program developer account and then click settings, switching to the specified mini-program within project details.
