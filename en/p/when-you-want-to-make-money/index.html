Ordinary people are social creatures, that’s right – you are a person, and also an upright-walking animal with strong social attributes; possessing feelings of inferiority and vanity. Society is constantly changing and eroding your essence. We won’t discuss the great individuals who willingly sacrifice themselves for society or for their country.

Right now

Considering average wages, or even those in my hometown, my current income is already significantly above average. What more could I possibly want?

Once you earn ten thousand, you’ll think of earning a million; once you earn a million, you’ll think of earning ten million. This is human nature, and one must confront their own inner desires.

What’s causing all the envy? It’s the easy money of short videos

Everyone understands that entering this industry isn’t easy. What you see is this short video, but you don’t see the scriptwriting and filming behind it. Yet, everyone dreams of being a genius, believing they are naturally suited for that field.

Begin contact

After watching so many videos, a little analysis reveals clear professional editing and a strong cinematic feel. This suggests they have formal training. While there’s logic to grassroots success stories, it doesn’t apply to most people, right?

On Douyin, you’ll find countless videos teaching you how to make videos. That’s when reality sinks in: if this could really make money, why wouldn’t they do it themselves instead of teaching others?

Anti-human recommendation algorithms

Previously, Douyin’s algorithm would recommend movie clips and anime edits, which were enjoyable to watch. But when I started looking into how to make money on Douyin, it began relentlessly recommending instructional videos, flooding my feed. As someone in the IT industry myself, I couldn’t help but wonder if the team behind the algorithm had lost their minds. Is this recommendation strategy meant to imply that users are foolish? And the fact that they keep suggesting ways to earn money through Douyin, from various angles and types… This piece is being written at 3 AM, and I didn’t even intend to write it. I just question how long this business model can last and how much time it will consume.

Understand life

Preaching one thing when instructing others, but failing to control oneself – that’s a joke. Not a pure technical blog writer, so I haven’t published much domestically. Here, I’m just casually complaining. If this gets shut down someday, I guess I’ll find another place. I can’t say Douyin hasn’t done anything; at least currently, it actively assists with the spread of real-time news and the promotion of state policies. After all, in China, you can’t go against the Party, right?

Thinking back to my school days now. When you truly can’t find the meaning of life, quietly reading a book is enough. In today’s era, how many people can still sit down and read quietly?
