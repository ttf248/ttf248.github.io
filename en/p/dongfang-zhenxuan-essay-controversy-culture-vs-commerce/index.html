Main body

The Dong Yuhui/Oriental Selection incident is a dispute over copyright and authorship, beginning on December 5, 2023, involving interactions between the livestreamer Dong Yuhui and Oriental Selection. This controversy reveals the complexity of business operations and prompts deep reflection on contemporary commercial culture and online society.

December 5, 2023: Event Start

  • Oriental Selection released a teaser video, and host Dong Yuhui’s recitation of a “short essay” quickly went viral
  • Dongfang Zhenxuan stated in video comments that these short essays were mostly created by the copywriting team and not entirely written by Dong Yuhui

December 13, 2023: Dong Yu Hui’s Response

  • Dong Yuhui published a lengthy article defending against stigmatizing anyone under the guise of “fancams” and clarifying his position on the matter

December 14, 2023: Management’s Response

  • Oriental Selection CEO Sun Dongxue released an apology video, admitting flaws in company management
  • Dong Yuhui also received an apology from Dongfang Zhenxuan Chairman Yu Minhong, who responded to the incident

December 16, 2023: Major Decision

  • Dongfang Zhenxuan has announced that Sun Dongxu is no longer CEO, with Yu Minhong assuming the role concurrently
  • On the same day, Yu Minhong issued an apology letter stating that blacklisted viewers would be unblocked

Dong Yu Hui’s New Role

  • XDF Education & Technology Group has appointed Dong Yuhui as Cultural Assistant to the Chairman of XDF Education & Technology Group, and concurrently Vice President of XDF Tourism Group
  • Yu Minhong revealed that he will partner with Dong Yuhui to establish a studio and launch new live streaming accounts and rooms

Conclusion and Reflection

This controversy is not only a dispute over copyright and authorship but also reflects the clash between culture and commerce. In this era of digitization and fragmentation, the ownership of content creation has become a topic worthy of deep consideration. The Oriental Select “short essay” incident is more than just a media storm; it’s a profound reflection on contemporary commercial culture and online society.

As observers, how should we view this collision of culture and commerce? While pursuing commercial interests, how can we protect and respect the creators’ labor? These are questions worthy of deep reflection for each of us
