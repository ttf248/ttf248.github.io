Trends and Challenges

  • The tariff suspension between China and the US sends a positive signal, but structural issues (such as high technology and energy) remain, and midterm elections may affect policy continuity
  • Companies are accelerating supply chain adjustments; Mexico and Southeast Asia benefit from “nearshoring,” but rising costs may curb recovery
  • The WTO dispute settlement mechanism is limited, and regional trade agreements (such as the US-UK agreement) have become alternative options, but this exacerbates fragmentation
