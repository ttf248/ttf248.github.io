Jiangxi Provinces Special Post & National Civil Servant Teacher Recruitment Data (2020-2025): Trends and Insights

Jiangxi Province’s teacher recruitment from 2020-2025 shows a significant reduction trend: Special post teacher hiring decreased sharply from 6,617 to 32 (a 99.5% decrease), and national cadre teachers dropped from 11,324 to 2,146 (an 81.1% decrease). While the proportion of core subjects (language, math, English) remained stable, the overall volume contracted. Though the proportion of arts, music, and physical education increased, absolute numbers were limited (e.g., only 2 recruits in 2025 for each subject). Policy shifts towards “one-in, one-out” staffing restrictions, with teacher resources directed toward vocational education and remote areas, have led to a substantial reduction in traditional primary and secondary school positions; some subjects had zero planned hires in 2025.

Jiangxi Province’s teacher recruitment from 2020-2025 shows a significant reduction trend: Special post teacher hiring decreased sharply from 6,617 to 32 (a 99.5% decrease), and national cadre teachers dropped from 11,324 to 2,146 (an 81.1% decrease). While the proportion of core subjects (language, math, English) remained stable, the overall volume contracted. Though the proportion of arts, music, and physical education increased, absolute numbers were limited (e.g., only 2 recruits in 2025 for each subject). Data Description

  1. Jiangxi Province’s Education Department and Human Resources and Social Security Department recruitment announcements and official statistics (2020-2024 figures are actual hiring numbers, 2025 figure is planned)
  2. 学科分类:
  • Specialty teachers focusing on compulsory education subjects in rural areas will be limited to a small number of core subject positions in 2025
  • National curriculum teachers cover all grade levels, with a more comprehensive range of subjects, including early childhood education and special education (not listed separately due to data limitations)
  1. 趋势分析:
  • The number of specialized teaching positions decreased by 99.5% in 2025 compared to 2020, and national civil servant teachers decreased by 81.1%, reflecting demographic shifts and adjustments to educational resources
  • 学科冷热:
  • The proportion of core subjects (Chinese, Math, English) remains stable, but hiring volume is decreasing year by year
  • The proportion of subjects like arts, physical education, and music is increasing, but the absolute numbers remain limited (e.g., only 2 students admitted for each of arts, physical education, and music in 2025)
  • Some subjects (such as English) have stopped admitting new students due to decreased enrollment
  1. 政策背景:
  • Implementing a “replace one with another” policy, strictly controlling new positions, and achieving a 68.6% proportion of off-budget posts in 2024
  • Teacher resources will be prioritized for vocational education and remote areas, with a significant reduction in hiring at traditional primary and secondary schools

Recommendations for Obtaining Complete Data

  1. 官方渠道:
  • Jiangxi Provincial Department of Education website (http://jyt.jiangxi.gov.cn)
  • Jiangxi Provincial Department of Human Resources and Social Security website (http://rst.jiangxi.gov.cn)
  1. 工具推荐:
  • Jiangxi Provincial Public Personnel Examination Website (http://www.jxpta.com): Downloadable historical job postings
  • Jiangxi Smart Education Platform (http://www.jxedu.gov.cn): Special Post Teacher Data
  1. 注意事项:
  • Some disciplines (such as Information Technology) are planned for 0 enrollment in 2025, but recruitment may be supplemented through transfers
  • Subject classifications may vary annually (e.g., merging “Moral Education and Rule of Law” with “Ideological and Political Education”), refer to the official announcement for that year

For more detailed subject position distribution or regional breakdowns, please contact the local education bureau directly or check the official recruitment system
